    Because we use 100% natural ingredients in our soap, and we can sell it at a lower price than natural handmade soap available in the market, so that your skin does not get harmed.
*   **Who will be your customers?**
    People of any age can be my customers. Specially health-conscious people who prioritize natural ingredients and wellness may be attracted to handmade soaps, also the local customers in the market.
*   **What have you found about your customers through market research?**
    It reveals that handmade soap consumers, particularly nowadays youngsters, are increasingly drawn to products aligning with health, environmental, natural, and cruelty-free values. Customers are seeking natural, chemical-free products.
*   **Why do you think the customers will buy from you?**
    Due to unique selling points, customers may prefer handmade soaps and customization options (e.g., scents, ingredients) and the quality of my products.
