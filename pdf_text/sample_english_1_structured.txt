## Product/Service Idea - Customer Problem & Resolution

*   **Problem to Resolve:** The product aims to address various skin problems (e.g., dryness, irritation) caused by conventional soaps.
*   **Solution:** Provides gentle cleansing by retaining natural oils, offering relief from dryness and irritation. It is made with 100% natural ingredients.

## Product/Service Idea - Competitive Advantage

*   **Why is the idea better than others in the market?**
    *   Made with 100% natural ingredients, ensuring no harm to the skin.
    *   Offered at a lower price than other natural handmade soaps available in the market.
*   **How is the product/service better than competitors?**
    *   **Customizable Options:** Allows customers to choose ingredients, scents, and colors.
    *   **Handcrafted with Love:** Highlights the human touch in production.
    *   **Small-Batch Production:** Ensures quality and attention to detail.
    *   **Cruelty-Free Vegan Option:** Caters to ethical consumer preferences.

## Product/Service Idea - Customers

*   **Who will be the customers?**
    *   People of any age.
    *   Specifically, health-conscious individuals who prioritize natural ingredients and wellness may be attracted to handmade soaps.
    *   Local customers in the market.
*   **Market Research Findings:**
    *   Handmade soap consumers, particularly youngsters nowadays, are increasingly drawn to products aligning with health, environmental, natural, and cruelty-free values.
    *   Customers are seeking natural, chemical-free products.
*   **Why will customers buy from you?**
    *   Quality of products.
    *   Unique selling points such as customization options (e.g., scents, ingredients).

## Product/Service Idea - Competitors

*   **Are there competitors/other options in the market?**
    *   Yes.
    *   Local small businesses and online sellers offering similar handmade soap products.
    *   Commercial soap brands.
    *   Natural and organic soap brands specializing in eco-friendly products.

## Product/Service Idea - Pricing Strategy

*   **Is there a price difference compared to competitors?** Yes, the price of my soap is less than other handmade soaps available in the market.
*   **How will the product/service be priced?**
    *   Prices are based on its unique features, quality, and benefits.
    *   Strategy involves identifying direct competitors, analyzing indirect competitors, gathering pricing data, and using value-based pricing.
